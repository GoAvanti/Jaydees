Jaydees Grass Trees is located in Melbourne's South East/Bayside suburb of Edithvale. Jaydees is a family-run business with low overheads so therefore our prices are much lower than most retail outlets. We are a boutique nursery outlet which specialises exclusively in the sale of Grass Trees. These can be more commonly known as 'Black Boy' plants.

We have a huge variety of both single and multi-headed plants ranging in size from small to approx 2 metres in height. We stock primarily Xanthorrhoea Johnsonii and Xanthorrhoea Glauca and some other species when available. With hundreds of plants to choose from, we are a 'One-Stop-Shop' and you are sure to find a Grass Tree to suit your needs and budget.

Grass Tree Sales

Prices vary per plant depending on trunk thickness, shape and whether the plant is single or multi headed. The most common plants are in the range of $4.00/cm to $6.00/cm of black trunk height measured from the soil to the base of the foliage. All of our Grass Trees are harvested under licence by Professional Operators and all come with appropriate individual Government Conservation Tags.
