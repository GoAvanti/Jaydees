Jaydees Grass Trees is located in Melbourne's South East/Bayside suburb of Edithvale.

Jaydees is a family-run business with low overheads so therefore our prices are much lower than most retail outlets.
We are a boutique nursery outlet which specialises exclusively in the sale of Grass Trees.
These can be more commonly known as 'Black Boy' plants.

We've a huge variety of both single and multi-headed plants ranging in size from small to approx 2 metres in height.
We stock primarily Xanthorrhoea Johnsonii and Xanthorrhoea Glauca and some other species when available.

With hundreds of plants to choose from, we are a 'One-Stop-Shop' and you are sure to find a Grass Tree to suit your needs and budget.
