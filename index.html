Jaydees Grass Trees is a family-run business proudly operating out of Melbourne's beautiful bayside suburb, Edithvale.

Jaydees is a boutique nursery outlet which specialises exclusively in the sale of Grass Trees, more commonly known as 'Black Boy' plants.

As family-run business we take pride in providing our value customers with service and affordability others can't match.

With our low overheads our prices are much lower than most retail outlets.

We've a huge variety of both single and multi-headed plants ranging in size from small to approx 2 metres in height.
We stock primarily Xanthorrhoea Johnsonii and Xanthorrhoea Glauca and some other species when available.

With hundreds of plants to choose from, we are a 'One-Stop-Shop' and you are sure to find a Grass Tree to suit your needs and budget.
