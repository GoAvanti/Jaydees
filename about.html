About Us

Prices vary per plant depending on trunk thickness, shape and whether the plant is single or multi headed.
The most common plants are in the range of $4.00/cm to $6.00/cm of black trunk height measured from the soil to the base of the foliage.
All of our Grass Trees are harvested under licence by Professional Operators and all come with appropriate individual Government Conservation Tags.
