About Grass Trees

Grass Trees are a protected species and it is illegal to remove them from the bush without statutory consent. All our plants are harvested under licence by professional operators and all come with appropriate conservation tags.
Xanthorrhoea, more commonly called Grass Trees or Balga (meaning 'Black Boy' in some local dialects), are native to Australia. There are almost 30 species found across Australia and here at Jaydees we carry Xanthorrhoea Johnsonii (featuring a blackened trunk and thin green leaves)andXanthorrhoea Glauca (a much heavier grass tree with strappy blueish grey/green leaves). Both are beautiful landscape plants. A long flower spear shoots up from time to time in both species. They are a slow growing, long lived plant which requires plenty of sun and very well-drained soil. EACH PLANT is sold with 'How To' instructions for planting and maintenance.
